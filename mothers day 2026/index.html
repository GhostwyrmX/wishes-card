A letter to you
May 10th, 2026
Dear Mum,

There are things I carry with me every single day that came directly from you — a way of laughing too loud in quiet rooms, a habit of caring too deeply, a certainty that showing up for people is never the wrong choice.


I didn't always say it enough. I'm saying it now. You are the reason I know what love looks like in its most honest form — not the easy kind, but the kind that stays.


Every time I am kind to someone, it's because of what you showed me. Every time I keep going when things get hard, I hear your voice telling me I can. You live in all of that.


Today is yours. Every good day, really, has been yours. Thank you for everything you've poured into us — it didn't go anywhere. It became us.

With all my love 🌿
My wishes for you

Everything you deserve this year

Rest without guilt

You've spent a lifetime giving. May this year bring you mornings that are slow, quiet, and entirely your own.

Laughter that fills the room

The kind that makes your eyes water and everyone around you join in without knowing why.

Good health, always

Because we need you here, and you deserve to feel as strong and vibrant as your spirit.

Joy that is only yours

Not tied to us, not measured by what you've done for others — just pure, personal joy that belongs to you.

To know how loved you are

Not just today. Every single ordinary day — to feel it the way we feel everything you gave us.

Always and forever —

We love you,
Mum.
